About Path Mate

Path Mate is a passionate initiative committed to enhancing the lives of individuals with visual impairments through technology. Our team is driven by the belief that innovative solutions can break down barriers and empower independence. With a focus on user-friendly design and advanced features, our visually impaired assistance app aims to redefine how individuals navigate and connect with the world around them. Join us on this journey to create a more inclusive and accessible future for everyone.

Key features

1. vission assistance 2. object recognition 3. face recognition 4. emergency sos 5. settings
